Core Beliefs

Not carved in stone... more like stitched on a comfy blanket.

1) Kindness beats cleverness

We prize being good over being right. Wit is welcome... cruelty is not.

2) Rest is righteous

We honor naps, sabbaths, and the refusal to grind ourselves into dust.

3) Share the snacks

Feasts are better when everyone eats. We run periodic food bank drives and “pantry tithe” weeks.

4) Curiosity over certainty

Ask better questions... hold fewer grudges. Dogma is banned for being boring.
