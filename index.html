About the Church

A playful experiment in community... taking kindness seriously while we laugh at ourselves.

Mission

Delight... then do good

We use humor to lower defenses and open space for empathy, mutual aid, and practical help. Satire is the hook... care is the point.

Scripture

The Velvet Testament

Short parables, absurd commandments, and rituals that translate to simple acts: feed people... call your friends... nap without guilt.

Practice

Micro-rituals

Weekly kindness challenges, 3-minute meditations, and the Rite of the Warm Beverage. Reverent silliness encouraged.

Core Beliefs

Not carved in stone... more like stitched on a comfy blanket.

1) Kindness beats cleverness

We prize being good over being right. Wit is welcome... cruelty is not.

2) Rest is righteous

We honor naps, sabbaths, and the refusal to grind ourselves into dust.

3) Share the snacks

Feasts are better when everyone eats. We run periodic food bank drives and “pantry tithe” weeks.

4) Curiosity over certainty

Ask better questions... hold fewer grudges. Dogma is banned for being boring.

FAQ

Is this a joke?

Yes... and no. We’re joking with the trappings... not with the values. We take kindness seriously.

Do I need to believe anything?

Believe in naps, snacks, and people. Debate theology if you want... just don’t be a jerk.

Where does money go?

Hosting, print costs, and a monthly mutual‑aid fund posted publicly. When there’s profit... we share it.

Can I start a local chapter?

Yes. Gather three friends, pick a coffee shop, and do one tangible good thing per month. Send photos.
